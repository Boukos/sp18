CSCI 8360: Data Science Practicum

Spring 2018

This course is a team-based practicum (students will be expected to have fundamental coding + machine learning knowledge).

Student teams will design, implement, and prototype large-scale solutions to real-world data science problems. There will be 4-5 mini-projects, and a final project.

Each of the mini-projects is modeled after Kaggle competitions. Team results will be submitted and scored, placed on a leaderboard, and ranked among the other teams' submissions.

Will your team be able to create the best solution? Register for the course and find out!
